Clinical context and equation

In PiCCO monitoring, a known volume of cold saline is injected centrally. As it mixes with blood, an arterial thermistor records a temperature-time curve. The area under that curve is inversely related to cardiac output.

CO = [Vi / 1000 × K × (Tb - Ti) × 60] / AUC

This demonstration fixes blood temperature at 37 °C and K at 1.08. It is for education only and must not be used for clinical decision-making.
